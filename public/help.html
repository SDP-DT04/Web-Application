Help Menu

Welcome to the DTO4 Web Application! A brief overview of the options available to you follows:

  • 'Overview' shows the 10 most recent workouts and polls the database every 5 seconds for updates.

  • 'View Swimmers' displays the swimmers and rfid tag pairings that are in the database.

  • 'Chart Data' allows the user to chart swimmer position versus time from a selection of the 10 most recent workouts.

  • 'Export Data' provides a tool to export data over a specified date range.
    Note the data ranges are valid from midnight of the first date through midnight of the second date.
    For example, the range 4/13/17 - 4/15/17 will export all data from 12:00 AM 4/13/17 through 12:00 AM 4/15/17.

  • 'Add/Edit Swimmer' is a menu for adding or editing swimmers in the database.
    If the swimmer does not exist in the database, he or she is created and added.
    If the swimmer already exists, his or her information will be edited.

  • 'Delete Swimmer' removes a swimmer from the database by name.
